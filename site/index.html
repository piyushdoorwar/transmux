What it does

Convert once, play anywhere.

FFmpeg powered

Solid conversion through FFmpeg, bundled with release builds so it works out of the box.

Wide format support

MP4, WebM, MKV, AVI, MOV, MP3, AAC, FLAC, OGG, WAV, Opus — video and audio in one tool.

Smart format detection

Automatically suggests the best output format based on your input codec — H.264+AAC → MP4, VP9+Opus → WebM.

Flexible subtitles & audio

Choose which audio and subtitle tracks to include, extract to separate files, or skip entirely.

Smart conversion modes

Stream copy for instant conversions or full re-encode for quality. Auto-detects when source matches target and skips needless re-encoding.

Conversion history

Track all conversions with timestamps. Quickly access output folders and review past operations.

Keyboard shortcuts

Power-user shortcuts: Ctrl+O (open), Enter (convert), Spacebar (toggle mode), ? (help) — or use your mouse.

Live progress

Real-time speed and ETA feedback while converting, with cancel support at any point.
